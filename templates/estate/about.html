Transforming Nigeria's Real Estate Landscape

Nestova was founded with a singular vision: to create a transparent, efficient, and trustworthy real estate marketplace that serves every Nigerian seeking their dream property. We recognized the challenges facing both property seekers and sellers—from verification issues to limited access to quality listings—and built a comprehensive platform that addresses these pain points head-on.

Our platform brings together verified agents, certified property developers, and serious buyers in a secure digital environment. With advanced search capabilities, verified listings, and comprehensive property management tools, we're making real estate transactions simpler, safer, and more accessible to everyone across Nigeria.

Our Journey of Excellence

From our inception to becoming Nigeria's leading real estate platform, every milestone represents our commitment to innovation, trust, and exceptional service delivery.

2020

Platform Launch

Nestova was officially launched with a mission to transform Nigeria's real estate sector through technology, transparency, and verified listings. We started with 50 verified agents across Lagos and Abuja.

2022

Nationwide Expansion

Expanded operations to cover 35 major cities across Nigeria, onboarding over 500 verified agents and listing more than 2,000 premium properties. Introduced our innovative property verification system.

2024

Advanced Features Rollout

Launched virtual property tours, AI-powered property matching, integrated payment solutions, and our comprehensive agent verification system. Introduced premium listing packages and property management services.

2026

Market Leadership

Achieved over 2,850 successful property transactions, 98% client satisfaction rate, and recognition as Nigeria's most trusted real estate platform. Launched our e-commerce store for home essentials and short-let apartment services.
